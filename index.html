Top Three Activities To Do At San Diego

Mission Bay

There's lots to enjoy, including several water sports with lots of rental equipment to choose from! Surfing, stand up paddleboarding, kayaking and more.

Balboa Park

Featuring 18 different museums, from the Air and Space Museum to the Natural History Museum to the Centro Cultural de la Raza there is a museum for the whole family.

Petco Park

Home of the San Diego Padres. With several locations to learn about World of Fame inductees, it also has a playground that features the tallest climbable bat in the world that kids can scale to the top.
